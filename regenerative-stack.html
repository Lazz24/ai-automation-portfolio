02 — The Players

Three actors. One region. Shared stakes.

Each is solving part of the problem. None is solving all of it.

Industrial Anchor
Tata Steel + Techport
IJmuiden, North Holland
What they do
Produce 7 million tonnes of steel annually. Operate the Netherlands' most ambitious industrial green transition, brokered through Techport's public-private network of 100+ companies, schools and governments.
What they've proven
15% CO₂ reduction per tonne since 1990. Top 3 most CO₂-efficient steel plants globally. €300M Roadmap+ environmental programme underway. €4–6.5B committed for full transition.
What the system still needs
100MW of continuously generated waste heat — currently vented — available as energy input for everything downstream.
Emissions Technology
KapiteinLabs
Haarlem, North Holland
What they do
Deploy pulsed plasma technology that captures and converts harmful emissions and pollutants at their source — without chemical additives, without combustion, without secondary waste streams.
What they've proven
Efficacy confirmed by Eindhoven University of Technology. Bluereactor eliminates ammonia from agricultural emissions. €1.6M secured in 2024 for final development phase.
What the system still needs
An industrial-scale deployment context — and a measurable output channel that routes cleaned emissions into ecological restoration rather than open air.
Ecological Restoration
The Weather Makers
Netherlands · Global Operations
What they do
Nature-based engineering at planetary scale. Restore ecosystems, reshape coastlines, rebuild water cycles in desolate landscapes — from the Sahara to Mozambique to the Polish waterways.
What they've proven
Active restoration projects across Egypt, Libya, Oman, Morocco, UAE and Poland. Already partnered with AI for climate resilience roadmapping. Proven methodology across six climate zones.
What the system still needs
Funding for established research — and a data layer that makes ecological impact legible to industrial and government funders.
Partial overlap.
No unified system.

04 — The Gap

Every complex system has an emerging need

This one is no different. One layer is still reaching to exist.

⟳
Cross-Partner Data Pipelines
Real-time data flows connecting industrial output, plasma processing, and ecological metrics into a single coherent system — currently disconnected.
▦
Unified ESG & CSRD Reporting
Impact spanning three organisations and two sectors requires a reporting framework that speaks to regulators, funders, and government simultaneously.
◈
Funding Narrative Construction
The Just Transition Fund, Rabobank, and EU Horizon each require different evidence formats. A cross-system translator is the missing link.
◎
Ecological Impact Monitoring
Restoration without measurement is goodwill. Restoration with measurement is fundable, scalable, and replicable across climate zones.
Without this layer, the loop cannot report itself. And what cannot be reported cannot be funded. What cannot be funded cannot scale. The three technologies are ready. The fourth layer is what makes them a system rather than a coincidence.
